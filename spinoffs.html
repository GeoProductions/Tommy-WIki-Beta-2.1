Tommy Minecraft

(2021 Spin-off show)

A one-series spin-off of Tommy that involves Tommy and Geo being transported into a Minecraft after a school trip mishap. They have to try to get out by beating the game, but will enemies block the way?

 

This show was actually meant to be S2E3, as S2 s answer to Kidnapped (S1E4), but a technical issue meant it had to be uploaded as its own show.

Road To Ninja

(2025 Spin-off show)

A spin-off about the show s two main ninjas, Christopher Long Potato Bauble Head Jones and Assassin Boldheart, diving deep into the duo s history and more.

 

It was announced a while back and has only had one episode so far. The second episode is set to release soon.

Catching Michael!

(2022 Promotional game)

A game made to promote Tommy where the objective is to click on fast-moving protagonists and avoid the slow antagonists.

 

It s actually a reskin of Catching Daniel The Game (2020), which was also developed by the Software & Gaming division of Geo Productions for YouTuber ENSTREEM , though that game has now been delisted.

 

It has received a few updates and there was also a planned re-texture of the game based on the show s new higher-quality recording methods, but to this day it hasn t been released.

Tommy World!

(2023 Promotional Minecraft Map)

Created to build hype for the upcoming fourth series of Tommy , Tommy World! is a digital Minecraft themepark featuring various rides and attractions. It has received some updates.

Tommy Fan Club

(2021 Google Classroom - Discontinued)

The Tommy Fan Club was created as a place for more dedicated fans of the show to hang out - it has since been replaced by the Community tab (formerly Forum ) on the Tommy Site.

Tommy Site

(2022-Present Website)

The place to be for the latest on Tommy and More! It includes a forum, blog as well as easy links to stream the various Tommy shows.

TommyPedia & Tommy Wiki

(2023-2025 Wiki websites)

In 2023, Geo Productions released TommyPedia , designed to be a free encyclopedia of all things Tommy . It was rarely updated due to its clunky design. It was eventually discontinued for a new Tommy Wiki That you re reading right now!
